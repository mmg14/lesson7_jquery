Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 bag semisweet chocolate chips, or chunks
  • 2 1/4 cups of flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375.

Put the butter in a microwave safe bowl, cover and microwave on medium power until melted

Whisk the sugars, eggs, butter and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth.

Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl.

Stir in the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients with a wooden spoon.

Stir in chocolate chips

Space the cookies about 2-inches apart on the pans.

Bake, until golden, but still soft in the center, 12 to 16 minutes.
